Features

Discover & Meet like-minded friends of similar interest and skills level

Our recommendation system suggests you groups, events to join. Simply sign up and indicate your sporting interest to meet like-minded friends, play and motivate each other to keep fit and acheive goals!

Track workouts and Achieve Fitness Goals

Keeping you motivated to achieve your fitness goals has been the main driving force behind our application. Complete Individual Goals, Group Goals and feel a sense of achievement with the encouragement from the community! SportsBud is compatible with fitness trackers and syncs data to track workout completion.
